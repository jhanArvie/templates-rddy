Our Story

What began as a small family dream in 2010 has blossomed into a beloved community gathering place. The Rodriguez family started with a simple philosophy: serve exceptional coffee in a space that feels like home.

Over the years, we've remained committed to ethically-sourced ingredients, supporting local producers, and creating a warm environment where everyone feels welcome. Our passion for quality and community continues to drive everything we do.

Today, while our menu has expanded and our walls have hosted countless conversations, our core values remain unchanged ‚Äì bringing people together through the simple pleasure of good food and drink.
