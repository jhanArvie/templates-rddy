Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions. If you don't see what you're looking for, please contact us.

A standard maintenance service typically takes 60-90 minutes, depending on the unit size and condition. Repairs can take 2-4 hours, while new installations usually require 4-8 hours to complete properly.

We provide free quotes for new installations. For repair diagnostics, there is a $89 inspection fee which is waived if you proceed with the recommended repairs. Emergency call-outs have a standard service fee.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and bank transfers. For larger installations, we also offer financing options with approved credit. All payments are processed securely.

We recommend servicing your air conditioner at least once a year, ideally before the summer season begins. For systems used year-round or in dusty environments, bi-annual servicing is recommended for optimal performance and longevity.

Our standard maintenance includes filter cleaning/replacement, coil cleaning, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting electrical components, testing system performance, cleaning the condensate drain, and a full system inspection with a detailed report.
