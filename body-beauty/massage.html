Our Unique Approach

At Touch With Faith, we believe in the extraordinary power of touch to heal, relax, and rejuvenate. Our massage therapists, who are visually impaired, possess an enhanced tactile sensitivity that allows them to perceive muscle tension and physical imbalances with remarkable precision.

This unique ability enables our therapists to provide personalized treatments that address your specific needs, whether you're seeking relief from chronic pain, stress reduction, or simply a moment of tranquility in your busy life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our massage services.

During your first visit, you'll complete a brief health questionnaire. Your therapist will discuss your specific needs and concerns before beginning the session. They'll explain the process and ensure your comfort throughout the treatment. Feel free to communicate any preferences regarding pressure or areas that need special attention.

For the best experience, arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. Avoid heavy meals just before your session, and ensure you're well-hydrated. If possible, avoid caffeine and alcohol on the day of your massage. After your session, drink plenty of water to help flush toxins released during the massage.

Our blind therapists possess an enhanced sense of touch that allows them to detect subtle muscle tensions and imbalances that might be missed by others. This heightened tactile sensitivity enables them to provide more precise and effective treatments, identifying problem areas and applying the perfect amount of pressure. Many clients report deeper relaxation and more targeted relief from our therapists.

The frequency of massage therapy depends on your individual needs and goals. For chronic pain or specific health conditions, weekly sessions may be beneficial. For stress management and general wellness, bi-weekly or monthly sessions are often recommended. Our therapists can help create a personalized treatment plan based on your specific situation and health objectives.
