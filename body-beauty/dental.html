Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, procedures, and what to expect during your visit.

Will It Hurt?

We prioritize your comfort above all else. Our gentle techniques, advanced technology, and optional sedation options ensure that most patients experience minimal to no discomfort during procedures. We'll always check in with you throughout your treatment to make sure you're comfortable.

What If I Haven't Been to a Dentist in Years?

You're not alone, and we're here to help without judgment. Many of our patients come to us after long gaps in dental care. We'll start with a thorough but gentle examination, discuss your concerns, and create a step-by-step plan to address any issues at a pace that feels comfortable for you.

Do You Sedate Nervous Patients?

Yes, we offer several sedation options for anxious patients, including nitrous oxide (laughing gas), oral conscious sedation, and IV sedation for more complex procedures. During your consultation, we can discuss which option might be best for your specific needs and anxiety level.

How Much Will My Treatment Cost?

Treatment costs vary depending on your specific needs. After your initial examination, we'll provide a detailed treatment plan with all associated costs. We're committed to transparency in pricing, and we'll work with you to maximize your insurance benefits and find financing options that fit your budget.

How Long Will My Appointment Take?

Appointment times vary by procedure. Routine cleanings typically take 45-60 minutes, while more complex treatments may require longer appointments. We respect your time and strive to maintain an on-time schedule. When you book your appointment, our team will let you know exactly how much time to set aside.
