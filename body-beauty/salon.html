Before Your Visit

Everything you need to know to prepare for your appointment and make the most of your salon experience.

What to Expect

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment to complete any necessary paperwork. You'll be greeted by our receptionist who will offer you a complimentary beverage and introduce you to your stylist.

Every service begins with a thorough consultation to understand your goals, assess your hair/skin condition, and create a personalized plan. Feel free to bring inspiration photos to help communicate your vision.

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a 50% service charge. We understand emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payment methods. Gratuity is not included in service prices and is at your discretion. The industry standard is 15-20% for services you're happy with.

How to Prepare

For Hair Services

  • For color services, arrive with unwashed hair (24-48 hours)
  • For haircuts, clean hair is preferred but not required
  • Wear clothing that's easy to remove or protect from color
  • Bring reference photos of styles you like

For Nail Services

  • Remove old polish before your appointment
  • Avoid lotion on hands/feet right before your service
  • Bring sandals for pedicures to avoid smudging

For Makeup Services

  • Arrive with a clean, moisturized face
  • Bring photos of makeup looks you like
  • Let us know about any skin sensitivities or allergies
