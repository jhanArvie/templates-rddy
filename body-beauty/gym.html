Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our gym and programs.

I'm out of shape - will I fit in?

Absolutely! Our gym welcomes members of all fitness levels. Many of our most successful members started as beginners. Our coaches are trained to provide modifications for all exercises, and our supportive community celebrates progress, not perfection. We offer beginner-friendly classes and programs specifically designed for those just starting their fitness journey.

What if I don't know how to use the equipment?

No worries at all! Every new member receives a complimentary equipment orientation session with one of our coaches. They'll show you how to use each piece of equipment safely and effectively. Additionally, our staff is always available on the gym floor to answer questions and provide guidance. We also offer beginner workshops regularly that focus on proper form and technique.

How often should I work out to see results?

For most people, consistency is more important than frequency. We typically recommend 3-4 sessions per week with rest days in between to allow for recovery. However, this varies based on your goals, fitness level, and schedule. Our coaches can help create a personalized plan that works for you. Remember, sustainable habits lead to long-term results, so we focus on helping you establish a routine you can maintain.

Do I need to bring my own towel and water?

We provide fresh towels for all members at no additional cost. We do recommend bringing your own water bottle, which you can refill at our filtered water stations throughout the facility. For your convenience, we also sell bottled water and premium reusable water bottles at our front desk if you forget yours.

Can I freeze my membership if I'm traveling?

Yes, we understand life happens! Monthly members can freeze their membership for up to 30 days per year at no charge. For longer freezes, there's a small monthly maintenance fee. Simply notify us at least 7 days before your planned freeze period, and we'll take care of the rest. Your billing will automatically resume when your freeze period ends.

Is there a cancellation fee if I need to stop my membership?

We don't believe in locking you into contracts or charging cancellation fees. You can cancel your monthly membership anytime with 30 days' notice. We simply ask that you complete a quick exit survey to help us improve our services. If you've prepaid for multiple months, we'll refund any unused full months. Our goal is to earn your business every day, not trap you in a contract.
