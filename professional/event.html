Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our event planning services.

How early should I book your services?

For weddings and large-scale events, we recommend booking 9-12 months in advance to ensure availability and allow adequate planning time. For smaller events, 3-6 months is typically sufficient. However, we also offer last-minute planning services for urgent situations – just know that some premium venues and vendors may already be booked.

What's your cancellation policy?

Our standard policy includes a non-refundable deposit (25% of the total fee) to secure your date. Cancellations made more than 90 days before the event date receive a 50% refund of any additional payments made. Cancellations within 90 days are non-refundable due to the work already completed and opportunities declined. We do offer the option to reschedule once within 12 months for a small administrative fee.

Do you handle destination events?

Yes! We specialize in both local and destination events. For destinations, we either work with trusted local partners or travel to the location ourselves, depending on the event's scope. Travel and accommodation costs are additional to our standard packages for destination events. We've successfully planned events across North America, Europe, and the Caribbean.

How do payments work?

We typically structure payments in three installments: a 25% deposit to secure your date, 50% due midway through the planning process, and the final 25% due two weeks before your event. For vendor payments, we can either manage them on your behalf (with a management fee) or provide you with a payment schedule to handle directly. We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment methods.

What if I only need help on the day of my event?

We offer day-of coordination services for clients who've handled the planning themselves but want professional support during the event. This includes a detailed planning meeting 2-4 weeks before, creation of a comprehensive timeline, vendor confirmation, and 8-10 hours of on-site coordination on the day. This service starts at $1,200, depending on event size and complexity.
