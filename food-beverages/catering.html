Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our catering services.

Yes, we offer comprehensive rental services including tables, chairs, linens, tableware, and decorative elements. We can handle everything from basic essentials to elaborate setups based on your event's needs and theme.

Our cancellation policy allows for a full refund of your deposit if cancelled 30+ days before your event. Cancellations 15-29 days prior receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations less than 14 days before the event due to food ordering and staffing commitments.

We take dietary restrictions very seriously. During the planning process, we'll discuss any allergies or dietary needs your guests may have. We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and most other dietary requirements with advance notice, ensuring everyone enjoys a delicious meal.

Yes, we offer full bar services including licensed bartenders, mixologists, and a range of beverage packages. We can provide everything from beer and wine service to full premium open bars. All our staff are TIPS certified for responsible alcohol service.

For peak season events (May-September) and holidays, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For off-peak dates, 3-6 months is usually sufficient. That said, we do occasionally have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to contact us even for short-notice events.

We offer complimentary tastings for events with 100+ guests once you've received a proposal from us. For smaller events, there's a modest tasting fee that will be credited toward your final balance if you book with us.
